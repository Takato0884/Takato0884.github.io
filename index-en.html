Takato Hayashi

林 貴斗
Ph.D. Candidate (3rd year), Japan Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (JAIST)
Research Fellow, JST Next-Generation AI Talent Development Program (BOOST)
Machine Learning Engineer, Dioptra Inc.

I work on personalized AI that can assist users in line with their own intentions and preferences. Recently, I am especially interested in flexible, general-purpose user modeling that can be transferred across a variety of computational aesthetics tasks, achieved by capturing users' essential aesthetic values toward photographs and artworks.

Keywords: Computational Aesthetics, Affective Computing, Personalized AI, User Modeling, Domain Generalization
